Receiving Address

How to get a Bitcoin address in 5 minutes.

You do not need to buy Bitcoin. You just need a spot to receive it.

You need a receiving address: a long string of letters and numbers that the SatScratcher sends your winnings to in the cosmically unlikely event that you win.

Path A: Cash App

Easiest for many US buyers. Open Bitcoin, choose receive, copy the Bitcoin address, and paste it into SatScratcher setup.

Path B: Strike

Clean Bitcoin-focused path. Find receive, copy your on-chain Bitcoin address, and paste it into setup.

Path C: You already know what you are doing.

Use any wallet you want. Self-custody is great. We are not going to lecture you about it.

One rule

Only paste a Bitcoin address. If SatScratcher says the address is invalid, do not override it. Copy it again.
