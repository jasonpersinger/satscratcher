What if I actually win?

First: you will not. Second: read this anyway.

If somehow a real block is found, the block reward is sent to the Bitcoin address you entered during setup. It appears in whatever wallet owns that address, like any other Bitcoin transaction.

You will owe taxes on it. We are not your accountant. Get one.

The probability of this happening with one tiny desk miner is effectively zero. Do not plan your finances around it.

The point of SatScratcher is not the winning. The point is that it is running.
