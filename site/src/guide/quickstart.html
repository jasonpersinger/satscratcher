Quickstart

Five minutes to a slowly-mining desk toy.

This will probably take less time than picking a WiFi password.

1. Plug it in.

Any USB port. You will see a pixel cat appear on the screen. Give it about 15 seconds. On first boot it broadcasts its own WiFi network.

2. Join its WiFi.

On your phone or laptop, look for a network called SatScratcher and connect to it. The setup page is served by the device itself.

3. Pick your home WiFi.

Choose your network from the list and type the password. The device will disconnect from setup mode and reconnect through your WiFi.

4. Paste a Bitcoin address.

Got one? Great. Do not have one? Here is how to get one in 5 minutes.

5. That is it.

The cat will start scratching. You will see a tiny hashrate counter climb. That is your lottery ticket doing its best.
