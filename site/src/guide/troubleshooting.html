Troubleshooting

The cat is being weird.

Start with the cable. It is almost always the cable.

The screen is blank.

Unplug it, wait 5 seconds, and plug it back in. If it is still blank, try a different USB cable.

I cannot find the SatScratcher WiFi network.

It disappears after the device has connected to your home WiFi once. To change networks, reset setup mode and try again.

The hashrate is lower than expected.

Normal. Tiny miners fluctuate. The number is not the product. The cat is the product.

The mascot looks sad.

The device probably cannot reach the pool or WiFi. Check your network. If your network is fine, give the pool an hour.

Something else is broken.

Email {{supportEmail}}. One human will read it.
